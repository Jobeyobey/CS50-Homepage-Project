Game Modes

Join the team!

Master different game modes

Overwatch offers a variety of different game modes. Battle in the casual or competitive ladder, which will see you fighting in Payload, Capture Point, Hybrid, Push or even Capture The Flag game modes. Alternatively, try out the arcade with a new ways to play every week!

Escort

In Escort, there is an attacking and a defending team. The attacking team aims to escort a payload across the map to the objective, while the defending team aims to stop them.

Control

In Control, there are three stages that get cycled through every round. All stages are symmetrical with a single control point in the center that both teams will attempt to capture. The first team to win two rounds wins.

Push

Two teams fight for control of a robot, which pushes a barricade for each team. The team who pushes their barricade to the end of the track, or the furthest along it before time runs out, wins!

Capture the Flag

Capture the opposing team's flag and return it to your team's base, and prevent the enemy team from doing the same to you! The first team to 3 points is the winner.

Deathmatch

There is one objective in Deathmatch: to reach 20 points before anyone else or have the most amount of points before time runs out.

Arcade

The Arcade has a rotating variety of exclusive game modes, maps, and rulesets.
